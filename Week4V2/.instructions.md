# Workspace Skills

This workspace includes two specialized skills for mathematical instruction and study guide creation:

1. **math-tutor**: Auto-triggers when you ask to explain applied mathematics concepts (PDEs, Numerical Methods, Complex Analysis, Real Analysis) with physics applications.

2. **study-guide-workflow**: Auto-triggers when you ask to create graduate-level study guides combining intuition, mathematical structure, and worked problems.

Both skills will be automatically invoked when relevant based on the keywords and context in your questions.
